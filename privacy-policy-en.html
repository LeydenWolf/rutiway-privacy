1. Introduction

RutiWay ("we", "our" or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

RutiWay requires access to your GPS location to:

  • Display your current position on the map
  • Provide real-time navigation
  • Create and follow custom routes

Important: Location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT sent to external servers.

2.2 GPX Files and Routes

When you create or import GPX routes:

  • Files are stored locally on your device
  • We DO NOT upload your routes to any server
  • You have full control over your GPX files

2.3 Storage Permissions

We request access to your device storage to:

  • Read GPX files you import
  • Save routes you create
  • Download maps for offline use

3. How We Use Your Information

RutiWay uses your information ONLY to:

  • Provide GPS navigation functionality
  • Allow you to create and manage custom routes
  • Save your app preferences locally

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

RutiWay operates completely offline after downloading maps. There are no backend servers collecting your data.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Maps

RutiWay uses OpenStreetMap maps, which are open source and free. When downloading maps, you communicate directly with OpenStreetMap servers, which have their own privacy policy.

5.2 Google Play Services

If you install the app from Google Play Store, Google may collect diagnostic information according to their own privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted local storage (according to device capabilities)
  • No transmission of personal data to external servers
  • You control all files and data on your device

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is on your device and you can access it anytime
  • Deletion: You can delete the app and all its data from Android settings
  • Portability: You can export your GPX routes and use them in other applications

8. Children's Privacy

RutiWay does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years old. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, contact us to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of important changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
